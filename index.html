We collect personal data that you voluntarily provide to us, including your electronic mail (email address) via our notification form, and professional roles or workflow preferences via our feedback surveys.

3. Purpose and Legal Basis

Your data is processed exclusively to provide project updates, announce our beta launch, and analyze market requirements for biotech data infrastructure. The legal basis for this processing is your explicit, revocable consent.

Data collection infrastructure is supported by Google Forms (Google LLC). Data is stored securely in accordance with Google's robust privacy and GDPR compliance frameworks. We keep your email for a maximum of 24 months or until you request its removal. Under the GDPR, you retain full rights to access, rectify, or request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten") at any time by contacting us via email. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Italian Data Protection Authority (Garante per la protezione dei dati personali).
